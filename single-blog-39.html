by Jackie Whetzel

KOURTNEY Kardashian's rarely-seen son Mason Disick has been spotted on social media.

The teen had a sleepover at his dad, Scott Disick's, house on Friday with at least one of his siblings, his sister Penelope.

FRIGHT NIGHT

Scott, 40, posted about his Friday night fun with his two oldest kids on Instagram, writing: "fright night."

He included a picture of Penelope and Mason, 13, lying in bed beside each other.

It's the first time Scott has shared a picture of his teenage son in several months.

In the photo, Penelope opened her mouth and gave the camera a semi-scared look.

Mason, who wore a black sweatshirt, sweatpants, and a spooky Halloween face mask that completely covered his head, held up his hands in a sideways peace sign for the camera.

'HE DOES NOT LIKE IT'

Earlier this month, Mason's mom Kourtney spoke about her oldest son's wishes to lead a private lifestyle on the Not Skinny But Not Fat podcast.

"I see comments all the time that I'm never with my kids. And I just don't love to post my kids on social media. They don't love it, sometimes they do," said Kourtney.

"Like Mason doesn't love it I feel like," she said, adding that: "He does not like it. He doesn't want any part of it. He's not on social media. He doesn't love it, and I want my kids to be kids. I crave normalcy as much as we can have it,"

Kourtney, 44, and her ex Scott share three kids: daughter Penelope, 11, and sons Mason and Reign, 8.

The Hulu star is currently pregnant with her fourth child, a baby boy, with her Blink-182 rocker husband, Travis Barker.

Travis has two kids of his own, Landon and Alabama, and a stepdaughter, Atiana, from his ex Shanna Moakler.

SECRETLY GIVEN BIRTH?

Scott's posting of his rarely-seen son comes in the wake of fans speculating that Kourtney has secretly given birth already.

Fans questioned the reality star's pregnancy status on Halloween, after the 44-year-old posted several photos and videos on Instagram that were recycled from previous years - but made it look like she was out trick-or-treating with her family.

MAMA BEAR STRUGGLES

It hasn't been an easy pregnancy for Kourtney.

The reality star announced in September that she had to undergo emergency fetal surgery in an emotional post on Instagram.

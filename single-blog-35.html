MERRY HAILEY! Hailey Bieber goes braless in tiny silk silver dress in elegant new photos for Calvin Klein’s holiday campaign

She rolled all over the ground in the low-cut blazer with sparks flying around her

by Jackie Whetzel

HAILEY Bieber has wasted no time putting the spooky Halloween season behind her.

Justin Bieber’s wife has gone braless while posing in tiny slip dresses and cozy sweaters for Calvin Klein's 2023 holiday campaign.

The iconic brand released some of the photos from its newest campaign, featuring Hailey and actor Brandon Flynn, on Wednesday morning.

Brandon, 30, is most well-known for starring in the Netflix hit show 13 Reasons Why.

Calvin Klein has shared several images from the ad campaign on X, formerly known as Twitter.

In one photo, Hailey, 26, stripped down into a tiny, low-cut silver slip dress, as she laid across the ground.

She was braless with neutral makeup and a touch of mauve lipstick.

Her chestnut brown hair was curled and parted to the side, and her white manicured hands were sitting gently on top of her head, as she gave the camera a sultry look.

BRALESS IN A BLAZER

In another snap, Hailey was braless as she posed in a low-cut, satin black blazer and sleek matching pants.

The brand captioned the post: "giving festive. for the party. Hailey Bieber in confident and sensual silhouettes."

In a third image, Hailey wore a tiny black dress with her arms above her head.

In a cheeky quip, Calvin Klein captioned the image: "Hailey Bieber is Giving Calvin Klein. what are you giving?"

UNDERWEAR SHOTS

Hailey's Netflix co-star, Brandon, also stripped down to show plenty of skin for the photo shoot.

In several black and white images, Brandon wore the brand's signature denim and underwear with his abs on full display.

It's not Hailey's first time modeling for the brand.

She was previously featured in a sultry Calvin Klein underwear campaign with her husband Justin, and several other celebs in 2019.

BIEBER BABY RUMORS

Hailey's newest modeling campaign comes days after the model addressed rumors of a potential pregnancy with her husband Justin.

The model has denied the baby rumors in the November issue of GQ.

"There is something that’s disheartening about, Damn, I can’t be bloated one time and not be pregnant?" Hailey told the magazine.

"It would be a lie if I was like, ‘Oh, yeah, I don’t give a s**t.’ "

She also said that she and Justin plan to keep the news to themselves as long as they can if and when it does happen.

"When there comes a day that that is true, you, as in the internet, will be the last to know," she said.

MARITAL PROBLEMS?

The Rhode skin beauty founder has been battling rumors of trouble in paradise with her musician husband for months.

As previously reported by The U.S. Sun, Justin was caught on camera dancing with other women at the annual Casamigos Halloween party on Friday.

His wife wasn't at the party with him and instead opted to go out with her celeb besties, Kylie Jenner, 26, and her sister Kendall, 27.

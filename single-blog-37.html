GROWING FAMILY Kylie Jenner debuts unexpected new family member in video at her $36m home after fans suspect she’s pregnant

Kylie held her hand over her stomach during the evening, suggesting that she could be pregnant

by Jackie Whetzel

KYLIE Jenner has added a new member to her family amid growing speculation that she's pregnant with her third child.

The Kardashians star, 26, adopted a new puppy.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder is already a dog mom to nearly a dozen other fur babies, including several Italian greyhounds.

The Hulu star posted several videos of the spotted brown pup to her Instagram Story on Wednesday night.

"You're so cute," Kylie gushed to her newest furry family member during the video.

The floppy-eared puppy was seen playfully rolling around on a white comforter with Kylie, as it nibbled on her hair and fingers.

PUPPY LOVE

At one point in the video, the reality star asked her new puppy: "Moo Pants... Are you done being crazy?"

She then told the puppy during a fit of giggles: "Oww, Oww. You're crazy," as the puppy lay on her bed while chewing on her hand.

The Hulu star gently disciplined her newest pet after it nibbled a bit too hard.

"No! You have to learn no," she said.

She then shared an image of the dog sitting sweetly on the bed with its big puppy eyes staring directly into the camera.

BABY NUMBER THREE?

Fans have recently speculated that Kylie will soon add another addition to her family - but they weren't predicting it would be a dog.

After she attended the Wall Street Journal Magazine Innovator Awards with her boyfriend actor Timothee Chalamet last week, fans were quick to spot a detail that suggested Kylie might be pregnant with her third child.

Kylie shares two kids, Stormi, 5, and Aire, 1, with her ex, rapper Travis Scott, 32. The former couple split in December 2022.

Kylie confirmed that she was back on the market and dating Timothee in September 2023, although sources said the couple had already been dating for months when she announced her newest beau.

Fans went wild last week after Kylie showed up to the WSJ event in New York City wearing a skintight dress from her brand Khy.

The Hulu star posted several pictures to her Instagram account documenting her exciting night on the town.

HOLDING HER BELLY

The photos were then posted on a Reddit forum dedicated to the Kardashians, and fans were quick to notice that Kylie held her hand over her stomach during the evening suggesting that she could be pregnant.

"Kylie cradling her stomach-- lil timothee?" one person wrote.

"Are you pregnant??" one person commented.

Someone else commented: "That’s what I was thinking. But she may have been bloated."

A third person wrote: "Do you think Kylie Jenner would ever take a selfie while bloated?"

A fourth person said: "Getting the vibe she’s pregnant again," while someone else commented: "Def preggy."

HER NEW BEAU

A source exclusively confirmed in April to The U.S. Sun that Kylie had been trying to keep her newest romance under wraps.

"Kylie and Timothée have been sneaking around for months, and she has mostly been hanging out at his house," the insider revealed at the time.

"Around the beginning of March, her security and car were spotted on his driveway, but the news hadn't leaked yet. She's been chilling with him in private ever since as they clearly get to know each other," the source dished.

Timothee was already a well-known actor when he started dating Kylie.

He's starred in a number of films including Dune, Little Women, Interstellar, and Love the Coopers.

The actor will star as Willy Wonka in the film Wonka, which releases on December 15, 2023, and in Dune: Part Two, which is set to release in 2024.

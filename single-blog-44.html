AGELESS BEAUTY Paula Abdul, 61, flaunts her age-defying figure in low-cut dress at CMA Awards after ‘flirting’ with Derek Hough

"How am I supposed to focus with this beautiful creature next to me," Derek Hough asked.

by Jackie Whetzel

PAULA Abdul has flaunted her ageless figure at the Country Music Awards in Nashville.

The singer, 61, wore the risque outfit on Wednesday night, the day after she was spotted flirting with the Dancing with the Stars judge, Derek Hough, 38.

TURNING HEADS

Former American Idol judge Paula turned heads on the red carpet in a nude floor-length dress that hugged her figure.

The low-cut dress had an intricate pattern of sparkly sequins all over it, and elongated sleeves with slits on each side.

She completed the look with a thick gold belt, gold bedazzled clutch, oversized earrings, and a pair of strappy silver heels.

While working the red carpet, the Straight Up singer took time to complement other female musicians and talk about the projects she has coming up.

"My idols are here tonight," Paula said, in an interview with Billboard.

She raved about Carrie Underwood, telling the outlet: "I'm so very very proud of her. She's handled her whole career with such dignity, grace, and she's relentless. I call her the bullet train."

She also complemented Taylor Swift.

"I went to the Eras tour and was literally blown away," Abdul said, adding that Taylor was magnificent in concert.

"She had the entire stadium in the palm of her hands. And to see her growth as an entertainer and her confidence... I'm just so proud of her. She's incredible," said Paula.

The singler also talked about her upcoming tour with New Kids on the Block slated for the summer of 2024 and a Broadway show she's co-producing, How to Dance in Ohio.

"It's about young adults on the autistic spectrum getting ready for their first formal dance, the 61-year-old singer said.

After walking the red carpet, she presented an award.

BLINDED BY HER BEAUTY

Paula's racy outfit choice came a day after she was spotted flirting with a much younger Derek Hough during her appearance as a guest judge on Dancing with the Stars.

Derek posted a video to his Instagram story showing the flirtatious exchange on Tuesday night.

After snapping at his sister, Julianne Hough, 35, to "Get out," Derek turned his focus to Paula.

"Are you ready," he asked the singer.

"My, look how gorgeous you are," Derek said seductively, as he and Paula drew their heads close together.

"How am I supposed to focus with this beautiful creature next to me," Derek said coyly.
